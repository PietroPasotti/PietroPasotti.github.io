Renew

at: 15 Jan 2018 on Speciale, Scheggia d'essenza, English

In the pale morning light
shrouding the ghosts
left impressed upon me
by my last dreams

I see my hands
they’re not the same
it was no dream
am I insane?
is that my skin?
is this a thought?
is it a yawn?
breathe in, sky-blue
refresh, renew.

It was no dream
as I wake up
I see the sleep
has shed my skin

Was I a snake?
Am I still one?
For panic’s sake
how has it come

to this.

There, left behind
like some old shoe
lies myself-me
refresh, renew.

Transparent shield
shrouding my bones
like slowly raising
morning light,
with fresher hands
I can now see
you

I thought I’d be
a different hue:
all mirrors lie,
refresh, renew.

I limp downstairs
you’re sippin’ tea
you have no trouble
telling I’m still me
so what is there
lying on the bed
where I used to be?

My skin’s new colour
its warmer touch
its baby-silk
like I had been
bathing in hot milk

my eyes’ new trust
like they can see
through the dreams I had
and the skin I was
I can bend the world
under my new laws

my feet’s new strength
they were made to run
and this back to bear
the heat of the sun

as I join your tea
my whole new self
is all for you
we can all see
it must be true
now I am free
refresh
renew.

To Maya…
